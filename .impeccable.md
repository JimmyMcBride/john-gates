## Design Context

### Users
Homeowners and design-conscious residential clients looking for custom cabinetry, remodel work, or one-off built pieces. They are evaluating trust, craftsmanship, and whether the builder feels experienced enough to handle a high-touch project.

### Brand Personality
Heritage, refined, trustworthy. The interface should feel calm, premium, and deeply crafted rather than trendy or aggressive.

### Aesthetic Direction
Warm editorial luxury with natural material tones, generous spacing, serif-led typography, and photography that emphasizes workmanship, wood, and architectural interiors. Avoid startup aesthetics, bright synthetic colors, and generic SaaS UI patterns.

### Design Principles
- Lead with craftsmanship and longevity before sales language.
- Use warm, material-inspired neutrals with dark wood accents.
- Keep typography elegant and editorial, with serif display moments and restrained sans-serif body copy.
- Favor spacious layouts, strong imagery, and subtle motion over dense interface chrome.
- Make calls to action clear without breaking the overall sense of refinement.
